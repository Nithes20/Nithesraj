History

The company was started in a garage in Gaiwadi, Girgaon, Mumbai by four Gujarati friends Champaklal Choksey, Chimanlal Choksi, Suryakant Dani and Arvind Vakil. They all belong to Jain families, and founded the company in February 1942.Amit Syngle, Managing Director & CEO of Asian Paints, India's largest paint and décor company, inaugurated the launch of the brand's boutique décor showroom, 'Beautiful Homes Boutique' in Bengaluru. The state-of-the-art store offers an enhanced home and decor experience that is sure make you want to revamp your homes.
